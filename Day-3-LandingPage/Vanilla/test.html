Rise Beyond the Walls

Humanity hides behind towering walls, clinging to fear and silence. But we, the Scouts, know freedom lives beyond the stone — where Titans roam and courage is tested. We do not cower. We charge forward, blades drawn and hearts united, for a future without chains.

Who We Are

Founded in the darkest days of humanity's fight for survival, the Scout Regiment stands as the spearhead of mankind's resolve. Where others see impenetrable walls, we see new frontiers to reclaim. Where fear holds the masses back, our mission drives us forward — into the unknown, beyond the safety of stone and steel, where Titans roam and secrets lie buried. Each expedition tests our courage, our unity, and our unbreakable spirit. We are more than soldiers; we are humanity's last hope, its vanguard of freedom, and its first step toward a future without chains. Every member bears the Wings of Freedom not as a symbol, but as a promise: that one day, these walls will fall, and mankind will stand proud beneath an open sky once more.
